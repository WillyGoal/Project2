Case Study

In 2017, the US Department of Health and Human Services declared the abuse of prescription opioids to be a public health emergency. In addition to prescription opioids, the use of heroin and of synthetic opioids is also on the rise.

Our team compared the prescribing rates of opioids to the rates of death by various opioids.
